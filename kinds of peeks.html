Kinds of Peeks

  1. Dry Peek - Peeking an angle without using any abilities. Eco/eco round: When a team chooses to save credits in order to have the ability to full buy during the following round and to destabilize the opponents' economy.

  2. Wide Peek - Peeking wide is when you peek an angle farther from the corner than you need to, to throw off your opponents aim.

  3. Crouch Peek - Jiggling is a basic strafing method and is great for peeking at critical angles in Valorant.
